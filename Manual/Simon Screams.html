On the Subject of Simon Screams

He’s angry! He’s furious! He’s enraged! He’s had it!

  • This module has six lights colored red, orange, yellow, green, blue and purple. These will flash in a sequence that grows longer with each stage. There are 3 stages.
  • At each stage, consider the whole sequence of flashes. In the large table, find the first applicable row and the first applicable column. From that entry, take the letter corresponding to the current stage and look at its corresponding column in the smaller table.
  • Go through that column from top to bottom and press every applicable color ((R)ed, (O)range, (Y)ellow, (G)reen, (B)lue, (P)urple; “#” = serial number).
  • Every time the sequence flashes again, your input is reset.
